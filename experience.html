Experiences

My experience in the study of a diploma of information management in UiTM Kedah for two and a half years which is a total of five semesters to finish my studies.First of all, I choose this course as my number one pick in UPU because my parents choose it for me and it is the same standard as my result SPM. Most people do not know what the course of information management is for including myself. For me, information management is designed to produce professionals who are conversant in the delivery of knowledge services in organizations and have the skills to manage information in a digitized format. The learning experiences will include among others, the organization of information, librarianship skills, database fundamentals, management of information resources including electronic and digital resources, and developing our information literacy.

Throughout the whole two and a half years of studying a diploma of information have to give me many experiences and challenges on finishing my study to pursue on a degree level. They are many things that I have learned from my life in university, lecturers, and my friends. There are when my friends and I able to cooperate and sometimes we had a misunderstanding in completing the assignments that are given by the lecturer. We were sometimes happy, laugh, stress, and even cry to finish our assignments together. Sometimes, I am giving up on my studies but there are my family and friends are there to support me and gave me some advice to light up my spirit, and I thankful for that. In my opinion, all things that we have gone through in this world are never easy but if we keep on going without giving up even though we face many failures in our life, then we might achieve our goals.
